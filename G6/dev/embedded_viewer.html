Embedded Viewer Example

The iframe below uses the following URL as its source: http://bit.ly/g6devembed
Long link:
http://www.geostor.arkansas.gov/G6/Viewer.html?embed=true&h=1000&w=1000&ll=34.7472185641546,-92.2906718809289&z=10&base=hires,acf
This is the building that the AGIO is housed in, it uses the WGS84 lat/long to zoom to it and changes the basemap to our high res imagery.
You can generate these links on the full viewer by right clicking the map and selecting "permalink", and by adding the &embed=true&w=1000&h1000 parameters, turn it into an embedded map!
